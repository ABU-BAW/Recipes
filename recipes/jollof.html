jollof

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved dish in Ghana and across West Africa. It's made by cooking rice with a flavorful tomato-based sauce,

often seasoned with onions, peppers, and spices like thyme and curry powder. It can include vegetables, meat, or fish, depending on preference.

Ghanaian Jollof is known for its rich flavor and vibrant red color, and it's often served at celebrations and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • water
  • Rice
  • stew

Steps to Prepare jollof

  1. First Bring water to a boil over intense heat
  2. Add Rice and leave to boil until it cooks well
  3. Add stew and stir for sometime!, voila you're done
